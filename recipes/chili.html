Chili

Clarissa's Chili

Try Clarissa's award-winning chili recipe next time you need something hearty!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (2lb)
  • Jalapeno (1) - Seeded and Finely Diced
  • Onion (1) - Diced
  • Garlic Cloves (4) - Minced
  • 2-1/2 Tbsp Chili Powder
  • 1 Tsp Cumin
  • Green Bell Pepper (1) - Seeded and Diced
  • 14-1/2oz of Crushed Tomatoes
  • 19oz Kidney Beans
  • 14-1/2oz Diced Tomatoes
  • 1-1/2 Cups Beef Broth
  • 1 Cup Beer
  • 1 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 1 Tbsp Brown Sugar (Optional)
  • Salt and Pepper - To Taste

Steps

  1. Combine ground beef and 1-1/2 Tbsp chili powder.
  2. In a large pot, brown ground beef, onion, jalapeno, and garlic. Drain any fat.
  3. Add in remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered 45-60 minutes.
  4. Top with cheddar cheese, green onions, cilantro or other favorite toppings.
