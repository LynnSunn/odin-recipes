Nonna's Lasagna

A savory recipe rooted in tradition. Continue the family tradition with this quick lasagna recipe! Using high quality Italian ingredients and some elbow grease, you'll make Nonna proud!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (2lb)
  • Sweet Italian Sausage (1lb)
  • Egg (1)
  • Onion (1)
  • Garlic Cloves (2)
  • Crushed Tomatoes (1 can)
  • Tomato Sauce (2 cans)
  • Tomato Paste (2 cans)
  • White Sugar (2 tbsp)
  • Seasoning Medley: Parsley, Basil, Salt, Fennel Seed, Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles (1 box)
  • Parmesan, Mozzarella, Ricotta

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven until well browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, parsley, basil, salt, fennel seed, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for 1.5 hours. Stir occasionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with an egg, parsley, and a pinch of salt.
  6. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  7. In a 9"x13" baking dish, add thin layer of meat sauce to the bottom of the dish. Arrange 6 lasagna noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread ricotta cheese mixture and top with mozzarella slices. Add another thin layer of meat sauce and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat the layering process and top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Spray a sheet of foil with cooking spray and cover the baking dish.
  9. Bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
