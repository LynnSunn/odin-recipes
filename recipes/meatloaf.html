Meatloaf

Mel's Meatloaf

For your next dinner party, wow your friends with this fantastic meatloaf with a delicious glaze!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (2lb)
  • Egg (2)
  • Onion (1) - Minced
  • Garlic Cloves (3) - Chopped
  • 3 Tbsp Ketchup
  • 3 Tbsp Parsley - Finely Chopped
  • Panko Breadcrumbs (3/4 Cup)
  • 1-1/2 Tsp Italian Seasoning
  • Milk (1/3 Cup)
  • 1-1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 1/4 Tsp Black Pepper
  • 1/2 Tsp Paprika

Steps

  1. Line a 9"x5" loaf pan with parchment paper. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, add all ingredients for the meatloaf and mix well to combine.
  3. Add meat to loaf pan, gently press meat down and shape evenly.
  4. Bake for 40 minutes.
  5. In a small bowl, mix sauce ingredients and spread sauce evenly over the meatloaf.
  6. Bake meatloaf for an additional 15-20 minutes.
  7. Rest meatloaf for 10 minutes. Drizzle baking juices over the meatloaf.
